Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Portfolio is a local-first application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Portfolio does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our end. All data you create — including your profile, portfolio names, descriptions, and images — is stored exclusively on your device.

2. Internet Connectivity

The app itself does not connect to the internet and does not send your content or personal data to any server.

If you choose to purchase a premium feature, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) through their platform. We do not receive, process, or store any payment or billing information. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details on how they handle purchase data.

3. Your Content

All images and portfolio content you add to the app belong to you. You retain full ownership. The app does not access, copy, or transmit your content outside your device.

4. Photo Library Access

The app requests access to your photo library solely to allow you to select images for your portfolios and profile picture. No images are uploaded or shared without your explicit action.

5. Sharing

When you choose to share an image or PDF using the system share sheet, the content is sent to the app you select. Portfolio has no control over how third-party apps handle shared content.

6. No Analytics or Tracking

Portfolio does not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or any form of tracking.

7. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally using your device's file system and a local database. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from your device.

8. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13, as no personal data is collected by us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected inside the app.
